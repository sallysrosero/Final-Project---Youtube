What Drives Clicks on YouTube Videos?
Project Overview
This project analyzes YouTube video performance data to understand what factors influence whether users click on a video.
Instead of focusing only on engagement after a video is watched, the main objective is to study the Click-Through Rate (CTR) and identify what variables may be affecting it.
The goal is to explore patterns in real YouTube analytics data and gain a better understanding of what drives clicks.

Objective
Explore what affects video CTR on YouTube
Understand the relationship between CTR and engagement metrics
Identify variables that may influence click behavior
Prepare the dataset for deeper analysis and modeling

Dataset

The dataset contains approximately 29,999 YouTube video records with 14 variables, including:

Click-Through Rate (CTR)
Impressions
Likes, comments, shares
Video duration
Traffic source
Content category
Watch time metrics
